- human birth defects : <u>소두증(microcephaly)</u>의 원인
- **Etiology**
- **Occurence**
	- 열대지방 : infants born with microcephaly 발생
	- 국내에서 소수 발생, 소두증은 발생X
- **Transmission**
	- Mosquito-borne Transmission
	- Nonmosquito Transmission
		- <span style="background:#fceef8">mother to fetus during pregnancy (수직전파)</span>
		- sexual/blood transmission
- **Clinical manifestation**
	- Acute febrile illness : 주로 <u>rash, fever, arthritis</u>
	- Neurologic complications : Guillain-Barré syndrome
	- Adverse fetal outcomes : <span style="background:#fceef8">소두증(microcephaly)</span>
- **Therapy & Prophylaxis**
	- no therapy (대증치료)
	- <span style="background:#e0e5fc">no vaccine</span>
	- prevention, control이 중요
