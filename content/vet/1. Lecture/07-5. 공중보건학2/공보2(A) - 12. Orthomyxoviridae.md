#### [[Swine influenza virus|Swine influenza virus]]

- 2009년 flu pandemic
- <span style="background:#fceef8">easily transmitted to human (vice versa: 역도 성립)</span>
- **Etiology** : swine influenza virus (formula **H1swN1**)
	- first isolated from pigs
- **Occurence** : worldwide, latent in pigs
- **Transmission** : aerosol, direct contact (handshake)
- **Clinical manifestation**
	- 증상 : <u><span style="background:#fceef8">high fever, bronchitis</span> in upper respiratory tract</u>
	- lead to <u>secondary bacterial infections, pneumonia</u>
- **Treatment** : Oseltamivir<span style="background:#fceef8"> (Tamiflu) - neuraminidase inhibition</span>
- **Vaccine**
	- available for animals / <u>not for humans</u>
	- human influenza vaccine은 swine influenza virus를 막지 못함

#### [[Avian Influenza Virus|Avian Influenza Virus]]

- 1997 홍콩에서 처음 발병 (H5N1) : 18명 감염, 6명 사망
- can transmitted to mammals(특히 <u>pigs</u>), <u>humans</u>
- **Etiology** : Avian influenza virus (H5N1, H7N7, H9N2)
  참고) <span style="background:#e0e5fc">H3N2는 동물과 직접적 관련X (사람)</span>
- **Occurence**
	- worldwide in birds
	- <span style="background:#fceef8"><u>latent infection</u> (특히 waterfowl물새에서)</span>
	- transmitted among avian species, carried by migrating birds
- **Transmission**
	- H5N1 : contact with <u>live birds</u> (<span style="background:#e0e5fc"><u>meat, animal products X</u></span>) → 사람에게 전파
